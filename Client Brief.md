### **The "Ad Strategist" Client Briefing**

Please provide the following information. The more detail, the better.

#### **1. Brand & Service Identity**
* **Brand Name:** (e.g., "Sleekview," "Blitz Window Cleaning")
* **Service Being Sold:** (Be specific. e.g., "Residential window cleaning plans," "Luxury Christmas light installation," "Pet-friendly synthetic turf")
* **Brand Personality:** (e.g., "Professional & reliable," "Friendly local experts," "Premium & luxury," "Tough & efficient")

#### **2. Audience & Problem**
* **Target Audience:** (Who are we talking to? e.g., "Suburban homeowners," "New homeowners," "People hosting for the holidays," "Pet owners," "Businesses")
* **Geographic Location:** (City/State. This is *critical*.)
* **Local "Enemies":** (What *specific* local factors cause the problem? e.g., "Georgia pollen," "Arizona hard water," "Houston humidity," "Oak tree shade," "Lake spray")
* **The Obvious Problem:** (The surface-level pain. e.g., "Dirty windows," "Patchy grass," "Tangled light strands")
* **The *Real* Problem (The Frustration):** (What does the obvious problem *really* mean to them? e.g., "Wasting their weekends," "Feeling embarrassed in front of guests," "Worrying about safety on a ladder," "Muddy paws in the house")

#### **3. Solution & Differentiators**
* **The "Dream Outcome":** (What is the "after" state they *really* want? e.g., "The best-looking house on the block," "Peace of mind," "More free time," "A clean, safe space for their kids/pets")
* **Unique Mechanism / USP:** (What makes you *different*? e.g., "We use a deionized pure-water system," "We include a protective nano-coat," "We are the only all-inclusive (install, maintenance, removal) service," "We clean tracks & sills, not just glass")

#### **4. Proof & Credibility**
* **The Guarantee:** (e.g., "100% Satisfaction Guarantee," "15-Year Warranty," "You don't pay until it's perfect")
* **Trust Badges:** (e.g., "Licensed & Insured," "Family-Owned," "Certified Installers")
* **Social Proof:** (e.g., "500+ 5-star reviews," "Over 1,000 local homeowners," "Trusted for 18 years," "Featured in [Local Magazine]")

#### **5. Offer & Call to Action (CTA)**
* **The Offer:** (e.g., "$100 off first service," "Free screen cleaning," "Free wreath with install")
* **The Urgency:** (e.g., "This week only," "Early bird discount," "Before spots fill up," "Limited holiday spots")
